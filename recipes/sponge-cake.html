Sponge Cake Recipe

This is a simple sponge cake that can be made in no time at all with only three ingredients. Choose your own filling for this easy, perfectly fluffy sponge cake.

Ingredients

  • 3 eggs
  • 1/2 cup castor sugar
  • 2/3 cup self-rising flour

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 190 degrees C (375 degrees F). Grease an 8 or 9 inch round cake pan
  2. In a medium bowl, whip together the eggs and castor sugar until fluffy. Fold in flour. Pour into the prepared pan.
  3. Bake for 20 minutes in the preheated oven, or until the top of the cake spings back when lightly pressed. Cool in the pan over a wire rack.
